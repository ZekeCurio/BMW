Technical Specification

Engine Type: Liquid-cooled, single-cylinder, four-stroke engine

Transmission Type: Constant-mesh 6-speed gearbox

Chassis Frame: Tubular steel frame with bolt-on rear frame

Rear Suspension: Aluminum swingarm, directly mounted monoshock,

Dimensions Length: 2,005 mm Width: 849 mm

Fuel Tank Capacity: 11 liters (approximately)

Performance Fuel Consumption: Approximately 3.33 liters/100 km (combined)
